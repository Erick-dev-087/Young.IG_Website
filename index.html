FAQ

Questions buyers ask before booking.

Why should I inspect a car before buying?

A used car can hide repair costs behind clean paint and a good sales pitch. An inspection exposes risk before you commit your money.

Do you inspect at the seller's location?

Yes. Share the vehicle location and timing through WhatsApp, and Young IG will guide you on availability and quotation.

What happens if the car has faults?

You receive a clear explanation of the faults, likely impact, repair priority, and whether to negotiate, pause, or walk away.

Can I use the report to negotiate?

Yes. The inspection findings help you discuss price from evidence instead of guesswork.
